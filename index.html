How I Work

My Design Approach

01

Understand First

Every project begins with deep listening — to users, stakeholders, and data — before a single wireframe is drawn.

02

Design with Intent

Each decision is rooted in user need and business goal. No decorative pixels — only purposeful, measurable design.

03

Collaborate Deeply

I treat PMs, engineers, and researchers as partners. The best design lives at the intersection of all perspectives.

04

Measure & Iterate

I move fast, test often, and refine — using AI tooling and systems thinking to accelerate without sacrificing quality.
